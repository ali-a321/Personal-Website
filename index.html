CoinCanvas - Crypto Portfolio Tracking Site
This crypto portfolio tracking platform allows logged-in users to search, add, and remove cryptocurrencies from their portfolios. Once added, users can document each coin's transaction history, purchase price, quantity, total cost, and date of acquisition. The interface enables users to edit and delete transactions. The user dashboard showcases total portfolio value, gain/loss, USD holdings, coin quantities, and percentage changes over the past day. It also includes an interactive line graph illustrating portfolio performance across yearly, monthly, and weekly intervals, along with a pie chart representing holding distribution. To enhance usability, users can sort their coin holdings based on USD value in ascending or descending order. Built using React, Node.js, Express, and MySQL.
Burger Bliss - Burger Website/Admin Panel
Burger website serving both customers and employees. For customers, the site features a 'Contact Us' form for valuable feedback, along with a user-friendly cart management system that allows for addition and removal of food items and item counts. During the checkout process, customers can review their selections, prices, and provide essential details via a checkout form. After submitting their order, customers receive a confirmation email with order specifics. On the admin and employee side, the system ensures instant notifications through websockets when orders are placed. The admin panel is secured by JWT authentication. The live order notification feature eliminates the need for manual refreshes, ensuring practicality. Admins and employees can also easily maintain an updated menu and food categories. They also have access to customer feedback. Built with React, CSS, Node.js, Express, and MySQL.
